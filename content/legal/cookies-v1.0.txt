   4. Why we use cookies and similar technologies
   Sing My Birthday uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

       To operate the service. To authenticate users, maintain sessions, prevent cross-site request forgery,
        route traffic, remember consent choices, support checkout, deliver songs or videos, and keep the
        service reliable.
       To remember preferences. To remember language, region, pricing tier, account settings,
        accessibility choices, and other interface preferences.
       To secure the service. To detect fraud, abuse, bot activity, suspicious generation patterns, payment
        abuse, and security incidents.
       To measure usage and improve the product. To understand visits, generation events, loading time,
        playback, downloads, shares, checkout drop-off, support events, conversion, retention, and pricing
        performance.
       To support adaptive pricing. To infer country, region, currency, billing territory, and purchasing-
        power tier, and to reduce abuse from inconsistent billing or location signals.
       To run marketing and remarketing. To measure campaigns, build audiences, show relevant Sing My
        Birthday offers, retarget visitors, attribute referrals, and test creative, pricing, and landing pages,
        where permitted by law and consent choices.
       To support raffles, reminders, and gifting mechanics. To recognise participation, avoid duplicate
        entries where appropriate, remember reminder choices, and support voucher delivery.
       To deliver third-party services. To integrate Stripe, Supabase, Resend, Vercel, Cloudflare, Google
        Analytics, Google sign-in where used, Meta/TikTok pixels, embedded YouTube/TikTok content,
        Anthropic, fal.ai, music-generation providers, and other infrastructure or product providers.
  5. Categories of cookies and similar technologies
  We classify cookies and similar technologies into the categories below. Essential technologies may be
  loaded without consent where permitted by law. Non-essential technologies are loaded only where
  permitted by law and your cookie choices.

  5.1 Essential / strictly necessary
  These are required for Sing My Birthday to function and cannot be disabled through our preference
  centre. They include technologies used for session security, account login, checkout, fraud prevention,
  consent records, rate limiting, delivery of requested songs and videos, payment processing, and
  infrastructure protection.

  5.2 Functional / preferences
  These remember choices such as language, region, currency, theme, pricing display, account settings,
  viewed legal documents, and product preferences. If disabled, parts of the product may become less
  personalised or may require repeated input.

  5.3 Analytics / performance
  These help us understand how Sing My Birthday is used, including visits, funnels, generation events,
  playback, downloads, shares, checkout steps, page performance, errors, and conversion. We may use
  Vercel Analytics, Google Analytics, privacy-preserving analytics, or equivalent tools.

  5.4 Marketing / remarketing
  These help us measure and improve marketing campaigns, build audiences, retarget visitors, attribute
  referrals, and show relevant Sing My Birthday offers. They may include Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Google
  advertising tags, social platform pixels, affiliate/referral tags, and similar technologies.

  5.5 Embedded media and social content
  Where we embed YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or other external content, the third-party provider may
  set cookies or collect device information under its own terms. We minimise unnecessary embeds and,
  where required, load embedded content only after consent or user interaction.

   6.3 Payments and checkout
   Where you purchase a song, video bundle, venue subscription, trial, or other paid product, Stripe and its
   affiliates may set cookies or similar technologies for checkout, fraud prevention, payment
   authentication, dispute handling, billing, and security. Stripe controls some of these technologies under
   its own policies. These technologies are essential to provide the payment service you request.

   6.4 Transactional email and delivery
   Resend or an equivalent provider may process delivery events, open/click events where enabled,
   message metadata, bounce data, and suppression lists for transactional emails, order confirmations,
   song delivery, account notifications, raffle communications, and support notices. Where email tracking is
   non-essential, it is governed by applicable consent and email-marketing rules.

   6.5 Analytics
   We may use Vercel Analytics, Google Analytics, privacy-preserving analytics tools, or equivalent services
   to measure usage, performance, and funnel behaviour. In jurisdictions where analytics cookies or scripts
   require consent, we load them only after consent.

   6.6 Marketing and social pixels
   We may use Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Google Ads tags, affiliate/referral tags, and similar tools to measure
   campaign performance, build audiences, run remarketing, and improve advertising. These are non-
   essential and require consent where applicable.

   6.7 Anti-abuse and security
   Where Sing My Birthday is protected by Cloudflare, Vercel, Stripe Radar, reCAPTCHA, bot-management,
   or equivalent providers, short-lived cookies or device signals may be set for bot management, fraud
   prevention, rate limiting, abuse detection, and security. These are generally essential.

   6.8 AI generation and media providers
   Anthropic, fal.ai, music-generation providers, video-rendering providers, storage/CDN providers, and
   related infrastructure may receive technical, usage, or request metadata necessary to generate, store,
   and deliver songs, videos, images, or related media. These providers generally do not need to set
   browser cookies for normal generation, but their APIs and hosted delivery pages may process technical
   identifiers.

   6.9 Future additions
   We update this inventory when we add or change a material cookie, pixel, storage item, or similar
   technology. Where a new non-essential technology is added, consent is required before it is set where
   applicable law requires consent.

8. Local storage and similar non-cookie technologies
In addition to cookies, Sing My Birthday may use local storage, session storage, IndexedDB, pixels, tags,
beacons, device identifiers, and other similar technologies. These may store information on your
browser or device, read information from your browser or device, or recognise your browser across
sessions.

Examples include storing your legal-document review state, language, pricing region, generation draft,
consent choices, account session state, campaign attribution, or dismissed prompts. Local storage and
similar technologies are subject to similar consent rules to cookies where they store or access
information on your device.



9. How we obtain your consent
9.1 Cookie consent banner
The first time you visit Sing My Birthday, or when our cookie practices materially change, we present a
cookie banner or equivalent preference tool. The banner allows you to:

   Accept all cookies and similar technologies.
   Reject all non-essential cookies.
   Open preferences and select category by category.
We present acceptance and rejection choices with comparable prominence and do not use design
patterns intended to coerce consent.

9.2 Granularity
You may accept or reject each non-essential category independently, including Functional, Analytics, and
Marketing where those categories are presented. Essential technologies cannot be disabled through our
preference centre because they are necessary to provide the service.

9.3 Records of consent
We retain a record of your consent or rejection choice, including the version of the policy and consent
interface in force at the time, to demonstrate compliance. The consent record itself is treated as
essential.
9.4 Children and consent
Sing My Birthday is not for direct use by children under 13. We do not knowingly seek cookie or
marketing consent from children under 13. Where an adult creates a song for a child recipient, the adult
account holder is responsible for managing cookie choices and consent preferences.



10. Managing cookies through your browser
You can manage cookies and similar technologies through your browser, in addition to the Sing My
Birthday cookie preferences control. Browser controls allow you to delete cookies you already have,
block cookies before they are set, and configure how cookies behave per site or globally.

Blocking essential cookies may impair core features such as account login, checkout, consent records,
generation, delivery, and form submission. Blocking analytics or marketing cookies should not affect
core product use.



11. Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control
11.1 Do Not Track
Some browsers transmit a Do Not Track signal. There is no universal legal or technical standard for how
websites should respond to DNT, and many browsers have deprecated the feature. We do not currently
change behaviour in response to DNT alone.

11.2 Global Privacy Control
Global Privacy Control is treated as a valid opt-out signal where applicable law requires us to honour it,
including for sale or sharing under California privacy law. Where your browser transmits a valid GPC
signal, we treat it as an opt-out from sale or sharing of personal data for cross-context behavioural
advertising to the extent required by applicable law.

11.3 Other signals
Where future technical mechanisms become recognised standards for privacy preferences, we will
assess and adopt them where legally required or commercially appropriate.



12. Changing or withdrawing your consent
12.1 Cookie preferences control
You can change your consent at any time through the cookie preferences control on the site. The control
allows you to update your selections at category level. Changes take effect as soon as technically
possible.

12.2 Clearing cookies
Clearing cookies for singmybirthday.com in your browser will remove the consent record we store. On
your next visit, the consent banner may be shown again.
12.3 Withdrawal in writing
You may also withdraw consent or restrict processing by writing to support@singmybirthday.com. We
respond in accordance with the Privacy Policy and applicable law.

12.4 Effect of withdrawal
Withdrawal of consent does not affect processing carried out before withdrawal. After withdrawal, we
stop the future use of affected non-essential technologies except where another lawful basis applies,
such as legal obligation, legitimate interests, security, contract performance, or fraud prevention.



13. Children and consent
Sing My Birthday is not directed to children under 13 and children under 13 may not create accounts or
use the service directly. The age at which a child can validly consent to cookies or similar technologies
varies by jurisdiction, including under the EU ePrivacy regime, EU GDPR, UK GDPR, and COPPA.

Where a parent, guardian, or authorised adult creates a song for a child recipient, that adult must
manage cookie choices, account settings, marketing choices, and consent preferences. We do not
knowingly use non-essential cookies to profile or market directly to children.
