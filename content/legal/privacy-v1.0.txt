02   About this Privacy Policy
2.1 Who this applies to
This Privacy Policy applies to any natural person whose personal data is processed in connection with Sing My
Birthday, including account holders, free users, paying consumers, gift senders, gift recipients where their
information is provided by a user, parents or guardians, venue contacts, venue guests where a venue submits
their birthday details, business prospects, partners, website visitors, social media users, and support contacts.

For most Sing My Birthday consumer and website processing, glomotec, Inc. is the controller because it
determines the purposes and means of processing.
For venue and business use, the venue or business may be the controller for its own customer or guest data, and
glomotec, Inc. may act as processor when it processes that data under the venue's instructions. Venue
controller/processor terms should be addressed in a separate Venue Agreement and Data Processing
Addendum.

2.3 Scope of Sing My Birthday
This Privacy Policy covers Sing My Birthday websites, applications, forms, APIs, email flows, social channels,
content-generation pipelines, payment flows, account features, venue tools, and any related interfaces that link
to this Privacy Policy.
If a separate written agreement applies, such as a Venue Agreement, Data Processing Addendum, partnership
agreement, or enterprise agreement, that agreement controls for the specific processing it covers.

04   The personal data we collect
The categories of personal data we collect depend on how you use Sing My Birthday. Not every category is
collected from every user.

4.1 Account, identity, and contact data
    Name, display name, and account username.
    Email address, phone number where provided, and contact preferences.
    Authentication identifiers, login information, password hash, session identifiers, and account security events.
    Age confirmation, adult-account confirmation, and parent/guardian confirmation where a song is created for
     a child.
    Billing contact information for consumers, venues, and businesses.

4.2 Song, birthday, and personalisation data
    Recipient first name or nickname, pronunciation notes, birthday date or birthday month/day, and optional
     age range.
    Relationship to the recipient, such as parent, friend, partner, sibling, colleague, venue guest, or other
     category.
    Language, country, city or approximate location, music style, tone, occasion type, gift context, delivery date,
     and reminder preferences.
    Personal memories, dedications, prompts, and other information you choose to provide for song or video
     generation.
    Generated Output metadata, including song version, style, model/provider, render timestamp, share link,
     playback/download events, and regeneration history.

4.3 Purchase, payment, and subscription data
    Plan, product, price, currency, regional pricing tier, discount, coupon, trial status, and order history.
    Stripe customer ID, checkout session ID, subscription status, payment status, refund status,
     chargeback/dispute data, and invoice records.
    Venue subscription details, billing contact, plan limits, founding-rate lock, multi-location details, white-label
     options, and account usage.
We do not store full payment card numbers ourselves. Payment processing is handled through Stripe or another
payment processor.

4.4 Venue, business, and partner data
    Venue name, business type, location, website, social profile, decision-maker name, staff contact, billing
     contact, and support contacts.
    Estimated birthdays per month, languages commonly used, preferred use case, venue branding assets, and
     pilot/onboarding status.
    Discovery call notes, sales pipeline stage, objections, pricing feedback, agreement status,
     renewal/cancellation records, support tickets, and venue-generated usage data.

4.5 Raffle, voucher, gifting, and referral data
    Raffle or giveaway entry information, eligibility, entry timestamp, prize/voucher status, and winner
     communication.
    Birthday reminder sign-ups, gift-recipient relationship, referral source, invite/share activity, and partner perk
     selections.

09   Children's privacy
Sing My Birthday is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. Children under 13 may not
create accounts, purchase products, enter raffles, or use Sing My Birthday directly.
Adults may create birthday songs for children only if they are the child's parent or legal guardian, or they have
appropriate authorisation from the parent or legal guardian. We may require additional verification where
required by law or where the product flow involves child-related personal data.
Where verifiable parental consent is required, a checkbox alone may not be sufficient. We may use methods
such as email plus a small payment-card verification charge, identity verification, signed consent forms, or other
mechanisms reasonably designed to confirm the adult's authority.
We do not knowingly market directly to children. We may market to parents or adult account holders about
products, reminders, gifts, or offers related to children only where permitted by law and consent choices.
If we learn that we have collected personal data directly from a child under 13 without required consent, we will
delete or restrict that data without undue delay, unless retention is required by law, security, or abuse-
prevention needs. Parents or guardians may contact support@singmybirthday.com to request access, deletion,
or review of child-related data.

12   Your privacy rights
Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion,
restriction, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent, opt-out of sale or sharing, opt-out of targeted
advertising, opt-out of certain automated decisions, and non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.
To exercise rights, contact support@singmybirthday.com. We may verify your identity before responding. If you
are a parent or guardian requesting rights over child-related data, we may request evidence of your authority.
We aim to respond within the period required by applicable law. Some requests may be denied or limited where
we need to keep data for security, legal, tax, contractual, fraud-prevention, dispute, or other lawful reasons.

12.1 California privacy rights
Where the CCPA/CPRA applies, California residents may have the right to know categories of personal
information collected, sources, purposes, recipients, retention periods, and categories of information sold or
shared; the right to request deletion or correction; the right to opt out of sale or sharing; the right to limit use of
sensitive personal information where applicable; and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising
these rights.
We do not sell personal information for money. We may share personal information for cross-context
behavioural advertising or similar advertising purposes where permitted by law. Eligible users may opt out
through cookie controls, privacy settings, or by contacting support@singmybirthday.com. If required, we will
provide a 'Your Privacy Choices' or equivalent mechanism.

12.2 EU/UK privacy rights
Where the EU GDPR or UK GDPR applies, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction,
objection, portability, withdrawal of consent, and complaint to a supervisory authority. You may also have rights
in relation to automated decision-making where applicable.
